Urgent: The Muggle World is Leaking!

"Attention Wizards and Witches,

Artifacts from the Muggle world are appearing in Hogwarts! We've found things called 'scissors', 'ebooks', and even a 'website'. We don't know what they are or what they are made of.

Your mission is to explore our magic subjects and compare them to the Muggle world. Watch out for any trolls in the dungeon while you work!"

— Arthur Weasley, Office of Muggle Artifacts

Magical Artifacts Guide

1. Magic Wand: Made of wood and a phoenix feather.
2. Invisibility Cloak: Made of magical fabric.
3. Time-Turner: Made of gold and sand.
4. Marauder's Map: Made of parchment and ink.
5. Remembrall: Made of glass. Turns red if you forget.
6. Sorting Hat: Made of old leather. It talks!

The Big Project

📍 Mission 1: The Comparison Scroll

Create a poster comparing 3 Muggle objects with 3 Magical artifacts. Describe what they are made of and use comparatives (e.g., "The Pensieve is older than a laptop").

📍 Mission 2: The Presentation

Present your scroll to the Class. Use your wand to point at the differences!
