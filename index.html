Hogwarts English Mission - Year 3

Urgent: The Muggle World is Leaking!

"Attention Wizards and Witches,

Artifacts from the Muggle world are appearing in Hogwarts! We've found things called 'scissors', 'ebooks', and even a 'website'. We don't know what they are or what they are made of.

Your mission is to create a 'Comparison Guide' to explain our world to them and understand theirs. You must also write down what happened during this discovery yesterday.

— Arthur Weasley, Office of Muggle Artifacts

Spells of Language

🧪 Comparing Magic (-er than / the -est)

Maths is harder than Art. History is the most interesting subject!

📜 Materials: "Made of"

The bin is made of metal. The ebook is made of plastic and glass.

⏳ The Pensieve (Past Simple Irregular)

PresentPastExample
GoWentI went to the library.
SeeSawHe saw a giant spider.
HaveHadWe had a geography class.
EatAteThey ate chocolate frogs.

Your Creation: The Explorer's Scroll

Part 1: The Muggle Artifacts Case

Draw 4 objects: scissors, bin, dictionary, ebook. Write a sentence for each using "made of".

Part 2: The Hogwarts Subject Tournament

Compare 3 subjects: Maths, Art, History, Geography. Write 4 sentences using comparatives (better than) and superlatives (the best).

Part 3: The Secret Diary (Yesterday)

Write a short paragraph about what your group did yesterday at Hogwarts. Use: went, saw, had, ate.

The Step-by-Step Mission

1. Assign Roles: Illustrator, Grammar Wizard and Time-Keeper.

2. Investigation: Use the school website or a dictionary to check materials.

3. Draft: Write your sentences on paper first.

4. Presentation: Show your Scroll to the class!
